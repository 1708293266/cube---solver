## English Short Post

I built an open-source browser-based 3D Rubik cube solver:

https://1708293266.github.io/cube---solver/

It supports 3D rendering, 2D cube-net color input, random scrambles, Kociemba solving, step-by-step playback, mobile browsers and PWA installation.
